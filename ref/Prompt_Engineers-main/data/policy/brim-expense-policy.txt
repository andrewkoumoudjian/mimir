Business Expenses It is the policy of Brim to pay for all reasonable expenses incurred by team members while doing business for Brim. You are expected to exercise good judgment with respect to any expenses you incur and check the accuracy of bills before paying or accepting them.   All expenses over $50.00 must be pre-authorized by your manager and receipts are required before any expense is reimbursed.   Once approved, the actual costs of expenses directly related to accomplishing business travel objectives will be reimbursed. You should use best efforts to submit receipts within the current month.  Abuse of this business expense policy, including falsifying expense reports to reflect costs not incurred by the team member is expressly prohibited. BUSINESS TRAVEL Supplier Entertainment: Reasonable entertainment of customers is acceptable.  Names of guests and purpose must be listed with the receipts. Unless dining with a customer, expensing alcoholic beverages is not permitted. Tips & Gratuities: Tips may be expensed, up to fifteen percent (15%) for services and porterage. Meal tips are to be included with meal claims and will not be reimbursed above twenty percent (20%). Parking: Reasonable parking expenses may be reimbursed.   Transportation Expenses: You are required to use the most efficient and cost-effective form of transportation given the total facts and circumstances of your travel. Tolls will be reimbursed.  Business travel using team member-owned vehicles is permitted.  Kilometres driven for work will be reimbursed at Canada Revenue Agency rates, which are subject to change annually.  If you are involved in an accident while traveling on business, promptly report the incident to your Manager. Brim does not pay for traffic or parking tickets, or for cars rented for personal use.  Car Rental: The Company will reimburse car rental costs when a rental car is deemed necessary.  If there are multiple Company team members at the same location, you may be required to share a car.  If business reasons dictate the use of a non-standard vehicle (i.e., four (4) or more Company travelers), you will be reimbursed accordingly.  You will be required to use your Company or personal credit card to rent a car.  Car rental, parking and gasoline receipts are required for reimbursement. Car Insurance: You should inform your automobile insurer if you use your vehicle for work purposes; this may increase your insurance premium.  Brim will not assume any liability for any loss or accident relating to the operation of your personal vehicle since it is your responsibility to ensure that you carry adequate insurance to cover such losses. If you rent or lease a vehicle, it becomes your personal vehicle for the purposes of this policy.

CORPORATE CREDIT CARDS Brim has issued corporate credit cards to select team members that are to be used for all business expenses when possible. Only the individual named on the card may use it.  Note: Team members are not obligated to obtain a corporate credit card.  Team members are personally responsible for all business expenses and will be reimbursed through the expense reimbursement procedure.   Use of corporate credit cards to charge personal expenses is prohibited. Brim may choose to restrict usage or revoke corporate cards where consistent abuse of the policy is evident. Brim will pay the fee for the individual corporate credit card.  The fees for other personal credit cards are not reimbursed by the Company. A lost or stolen credit card must be reported immediately to the card issuer. 
Company Events We ask that all team members exercise common sense and good judgment during all Company events, and we remind you that while we encourage a festive atmosphere, these are business events, and all Company policies regarding team member behaviour are still applicable. Note that Brim supports the policy of “Don’t Drink and Drive” and will make all reasonable efforts to ensure that team members not drive while in an impaired condition. As a matter of policy, Brim forbids any team member from driving while impaired following any work function. Brim strongly recommends that team members who consume alcohol at Company events pre-arrange for their transportation home. If necessary, the Company shall arrange and/or pay for transportation of the team member to their home. 
Company Property Preserving and safeguarding Brim’s property is the responsibility of each team member. Equipment, materials, and supplies are the property of Brim and must be used only for Company business on Company premises or on work sites. All such equipment, materials, and supplies must be protected from theft, misuse, or damage.  Team members may be disciplined for loss or damage of Company property due to negligence. No Company property may be borrowed for personal use without the authorization of management. Theft of Brim’s property or of a co-worker’s property may result in criminal charges. All Company-owned assets, including but not limited to laptops, smartphones and electronic passes, must be returned upon notification of termination or resignation.
