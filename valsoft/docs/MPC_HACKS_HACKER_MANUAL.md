## Rules of Hacking

1. Teams of up to **4 people**.
2. Projects must be completed and submitted to **Devpost by 11:30 AM Sunday, May 31st** (details to follow).
3. Link your **public GitHub repository** to your submission.
4. No projects from other events; one submission per team.
5. Official time zone: **Montreal, QC (UTC-5)**.
